## **Create an Ad place**

Entering the ad slots management and then creating ad slots. Name the ad slot with lowercase letters **`a-z`** , number **`0-9`** and underscore **`_`** .\
AotterTrek currently provides three types of ad and different sizes:

* **Supr.Ad (336x280)**
* **Supr.Ad (300x250)**
* **Banner Ad (640x100)**
